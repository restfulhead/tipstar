About

Tip Star is a simple percentage calculator that let’s you easily and instantly calculate percentages. Calculating discount, tax, or tip are just three of many examples where percentage calculation can help everyday.

The “apply” mode allows you to enter an amount and a percentage value. Different results based on your input are shown immediately. Besides adding (e.g. to calculate tips) and subtracting (discounts anyone?), it also calculates increase and reduction figures (would you like to know how much VAT you paid in Europe?). You can also easily divide the result by a number you specify. So splitting the bill becomes a little bit more easy.

The “compare” mode works similar, but allows you to enter two amounts that the tool then compares. For example you could easily check how much they increased the rent this year again.

The responsive app works on computers, tablets and mobile devices. The interface automatically adapts to provide you with the best user experience depending on your available screen size.

Not online? Not a problem. Tip Star.us uses the new HTML5 offline feature, so that you can use the app whenever you need it.

And best of it all? It’s absolutely free to use.
